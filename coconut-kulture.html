✦ Our Story

Where Heritage Meets Modern Artistry

Born from a deep love of Thai culinary traditions, Siam Aura was founded in 2012 by Chef Niran Thong, who trained under Bangkok's most celebrated kitchen masters before bringing these recipes to the shores of Australia.

Each dish honours the four pillars of Thai cooking — spicy, sour, sweet, and salty — balanced with the precision of a craftsman and the soul of a storyteller. We source our herbs fresh each morning and mill our curry pastes by hand, just as they have been for centuries.

✦ Meet the Artist

Chef Niran Thong

Executive Chef & Founder

With 20 years of culinary mastery spanning Bangkok, Singapore and Sydney, Chef Niran's cuisine tells the story of Thailand's rich and diverse food culture — from the floating markets of Bangkok to the hill tribes of northern Chiang Mai. His philosophy is simple: respect the ingredient, honour the tradition, and cook with love.
